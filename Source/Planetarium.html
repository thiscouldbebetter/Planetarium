Planetarium*

Enter a set of coordinates for stars, then the observer's latitude, longitude, heading, and pitch, plus the date and time of the observation, then click the Render button to get a view of the night sky at that time and location.









[none]

*"Planetarium" is a bit of a misnomer, since this program will only accurately render objects so far from the Earth that their position in the celestial sphere seems "fixed", which is true of stars but not planets.
